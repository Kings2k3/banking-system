Common Questions

Frequently asked
questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from our customers.

Is my money safe with Payvexis?

Absolutely. All deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000. We use 256-bit AES encryption, AI-powered fraud detection, and 24/7 real-time transaction monitoring. Your money and data are protected by multiple layers of bank-grade security.

How fast can I get help?

Our average live chat response time is under 2 minutes. Phone support connects you instantly — no menus, no hold music. Email responses arrive within 4 hours, guaranteed.

What happens if I see an unauthorized transaction?

You're covered by our zero liability fraud protection. Report the transaction through the app or contact us, and we'll freeze the charge immediately, investigate, and refund you — typically within 24 hours.

Are there any hidden fees?

Never. Payvexis has a transparent fee structure. No maintenance fees, no minimum balance fees, and no surprise charges. All applicable fees are clearly stated before you confirm any transaction.

How do I open an account?

Opening an account takes under 5 minutes. Download the app or tap "Open Account" on our website, verify your identity with a quick selfie and ID scan, and you're ready to go. No paperwork, no branch visits required.

What regulatory bodies oversee Payvexis?

Payvexis is regulated by the OCC (Office of the Comptroller of the Currency), SEC, and FINRA. We are PCI DSS Level 1 certified, SOC 2 Type II audited, GDPR compliant, and ISO 27001 certified.
