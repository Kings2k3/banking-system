Common Questions

Frequently asked
questions

How fast can I get a Payvexis card?

Virtual cards are issued instantly after identity verification. Physical cards are shipped within 3-5 business days and arrive with contactless capabilities already activated.

Can I freeze my card if it's lost?

Yes. You can instantly freeze any card from the Payvexis app. All outgoing transactions are paused immediately. You can unfreeze just as quickly if you find it.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. Standard and virtual cards have zero monthly fees. The Premium Metal card is $9.99/month and includes all premium perks. All fees are transparently shown before you confirm any transaction.

How is my card data protected?

All card data is encrypted with 256-bit AES encryption. Card numbers are tokenized for contactless and online payments. We use AI-based fraud detection that monitors every transaction in real-time, 24/7.
